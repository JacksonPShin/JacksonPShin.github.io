Debating

Another one of my skills is debating, and it actually comes in handy a lot. I’m good at making strong points and backing them up with reasons, so I can usually convince people (like my parents). Sometimes, though, I end up using it to annoy them a little by arguing my side, even if they don’t agree. But hey, debating is all about getting your point across, right?

Coding

Coding is one of my skills, and right now, I'm using it to make a game kind of like the old Super Mario Brothers. It's super hard, but also really fun. There’s a lot of stuff to figure out, like how to make the game work and fix bugs, but it feels awesome when everything starts coming together. Even though it’s tough sometimes, I enjoy the challenge and learning new things along the way. It’s cool to see my ideas turn into something real with coding!

Fixing things

One of my skills is fixing things, and it actually ties in with coding. When something breaks, like a gadget or my code, I like figuring out what went wrong and fixing it. It’s kind of like solving a puzzle. In coding, I’m always hunting down bugs and errors, and when I finally fix them, it feels awesome. It’s cool how both fixing things and coding are about finding problems and figuring out how to solve them!
